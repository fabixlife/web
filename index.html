TRASHTERROR?...

Welcome to TrashTerror, where your choices shape the digital environment. Dispose of virtual waste responsibly, as unchecked pollution in the game can lead to adverse impacts. Irresponsible waste disposal may trigger virtual ecological crises, causing habitat destruction, declining biodiversity, and compromising virtual health.

Our aim

The aim of TrashTerror is to engage players in an interactive experience that raises awareness about waste pollution. Through responsible decision-making within the virtual environment, players strive to minimize their virtual carbon footprint, mitigate the impacts of pollution, and foster a sustainable and thriving digital ecosystem.

Why us?

The game encourages players to adopt eco-friendly practices, showcasing the importance of responsible waste management and sustainable choices in both the virtual world and their everyday lives.

Our Vision

Their vision extends beyond entertainment, aiming to inspire positive real-world action by fostering a sense of responsibility and environmental consciousness among players. The developers of TrashTerror aspire to demonstrate the transformative power of gaming as a tool for education and advocacy,
